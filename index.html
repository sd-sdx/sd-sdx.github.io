    TAKE YOUR SUPPORT SKILLS TO THE NEXT LEVEL

    July 22, 2017

    Portland, Oregon

    We set out to create an event that works for everyone in Support - from folks on the front lines to managers and up.

    SDX is focused on learning and developing skills in customer support, management, self development, and more. These skills are brought to you through a variety of workshops and talks with support situations and perspective in mind, so you can apply the skills right away.

    If one of these sessions could benefit your career or your job, you should be here.
